### T Gate

This gate applies a rotation of $\frac{\pi}{4}$ radians about the $z$-axis to a single qubit, incuding a $\frac{\pi}{4}$ phase.

It is equivalent to the following:
$$
\text{T} = \text{P}\left( \frac{\pi}{4} \right) = \sqrt{\text{S}} = \text{Z}^{\frac{1}{4}}
$$
See the [P](/gates/phase), [S](/gates/s) and [Z](/gates/z) gates.
