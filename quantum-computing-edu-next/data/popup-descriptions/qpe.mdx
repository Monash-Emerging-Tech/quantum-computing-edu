### Algorithm Overview

Quantum Phase Estimation is a quantum circuit that solves the phase estimation problem in a quantum circuit.

QPE can be used to find the spectral decomposition of a unitary matrix, encoding its eigenvalues into binary format.


### Phase estimation problem
We are given, as input, a description of a unitary quantum operation $U$ that operates on $n$ qubits,
and an $n$-qubit quantum state $\ket{\psi}$. We are promised that $\ket{\psi}$ is an eigenvector of $U$.

The output is an approximation of $\theta \in \left[ 0, 1 \right)$ satisfying $U \ket{\psi} = e^{2 \pi i \theta} \ket{\psi}$.
That is, $e^{2 \pi i \theta}$ is an eigenvalue of $U$ corresponding to the eigenvector $\ket{\psi}$.
