### Phase Gate

This gate applies a rotation of $\theta$ radians about the $z$-axis to a single qubit.

It is equivalent to [RZ](./rz) up to a phase factor:
$$
\text{P}(\theta) = e^{i\frac{\theta}{2}}\text{RZ}\left( \theta \right)
$$


### Parameters

$\theta \in \mathbb{R}$: Angle to rotate the state by, in radians.
