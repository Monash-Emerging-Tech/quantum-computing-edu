### RZ Gate

This gate applies a rotation of $\phi$ radians about the $z$-axis to a single qubit.

It can also be defined by:
$$
\text{RZ}(\phi) = \text{exp}\left( -i \frac{\phi}{2} Z \right)
$$
where $Z$ is the [Pauli Z gate](./z).


### Parameters

$\phi \in \mathbb{R}$:  Angle to rotate the state by, in radians.
