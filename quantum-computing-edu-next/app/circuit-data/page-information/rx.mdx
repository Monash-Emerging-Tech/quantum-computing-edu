### RX Gate

This gate applies a rotation of $\theta$ radians about the $x$-axis to a single qubit.

It can also be defined by:
$$
\text{RX}(\theta) = \text{exp}\left( -i \frac{\theta}{2} X \right)
$$
where $X$ is the [Pauli X gate](./x).


### Parameters

$\theta \in \mathbb{R}$: Angle to rotate the state by, in radians.
